The Life and Career of Alix Earle

Alix Earle is a popular social media influencer known for her lifestyle, beauty, and “Get Ready With Me” videos on TikTok and Instagram. Her relatable personality and openness about everyday life helped her gain millions of followers online.

Early Life

Alix Earle grew up in New Jersey and later attended the University of Miami. During college she began posting videos about her daily routines, fashion, and beauty tips. Her authentic personality quickly attracted attention from viewers on TikTok.

Rise on Social Media

Alix gained popularity through her “Get Ready With Me” videos where she talks about her day while doing her makeup. Her honesty about topics like acne and college life made her stand out from many other influencers.

As her audience grew, she partnered with several brands and became one of the most talked-about lifestyle creators on TikTok.

About Alix Earle

Alix Earle is a social media influencer from New Jersey, known for her TikTok and Instagram content. She creates videos about lifestyle, beauty, and daily life that feel relatable and fun. As of 2026, she has millions of followers and is admired for her authentic personality and honest storytelling.

How It Started

Alix Earle began gaining attention while she was a student at the University of Miami. Her content focuses on beauty, lifestyle, and everyday moments that resonate with young adults. Her relatable energy helped her grow quickly on social media.

Fans often participate in her trending challenge #AlixEarleChallenge on TikTok.
